Why BantayManila?

BantayManila is a platform that helps residents report and track community issues. It connects citizens with local authorities to ensure concerns are seen and resolved.

📋
Easy Reporting

Quickly submit issues with clear details, photos, and exact location to help authorities understand the problem better.

Real-Time Updates

Stay informed by tracking the progress of your reports and receiving updates as actions are taken.

🤝
Community Driven

Work together with other residents to highlight, support, and prioritize important community issues.

Our
Mission

To empower residents to easily report community issues and ensure they are tracked and resolved. We aim to promote transparency, faster response, and a cleaner, safer Manila through technology.

Key Features

  • Easy Reporting – Submit issues with description, photo, and location.
  • Track Progress – See updates from pending to resolved.
  • Interactive Map – View all reported issues across Manila.
  • Notifications – Get alerts when your report is updated.
  • Community Support – Others can confirm or upvote issues.
